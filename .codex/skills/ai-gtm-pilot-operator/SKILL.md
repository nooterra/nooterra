# AI GTM Pilot Operator

## Use this skill when

- You need vertical sequencing and pilot-market fit plans.
- You need customer-facing rollout assets and KPI definitions.
- You need expansion motions from pilot to recurring revenue.

## Workflow

1. Select target segment with urgency and budget fit.
2. Define pilot package (scope, timeline, success metrics).
3. Map onboarding steps to product and engineering dependencies.
4. Produce expansion criteria and conversion triggers.

## Output contract

- Pilot plan with target accounts and timeline.
- Success scorecard with leading/lagging indicators.
- Expansion playbook for post-pilot conversion.
